Welcome to the Turn-Based Combat Game! In this game, you will face an enemy in a turn-based battle. You and the enemy will take turns rolling a dice to determine who attacks first. During each turn, you will have the chance to attack the enemy or be attacked by the enemy. The game will continue until either you or the enemy runs out of health. Good luck, and may the odds be ever in your favor!
